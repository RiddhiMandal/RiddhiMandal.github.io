About me

Who am I?

Hi, I'm Riddhi Mandal, a Research Assistant at Hong Kong Metropolitan University. I am deeply involved in a collaborative project with The Education University of Hong Kong focusing on biomechanics under Prof. Kevin Hung and Prof. Daniel Chow. Through this collaboration, my passion for leveraging machine learning, deep learning, and human-computer interaction to solve real-world problems is further enriched.

Before joining MU, I was involved in AI-based research projects as an intern at different labs. At G H Raisoni College of Engineering, I was a Biotechnology Industry Research Assistance Council (BIRAC) Junior Research Fellow under Dr. Vibha Bora in Biomedical Engineering & Technology Innovation Centre (BETiC) lab. Following this, I've gained valuable experience as a MITACS Globalink Research Intern at the University of Northern British Columbia under Dr. Jianhui Zhou at Wood Innovation and Design Centre, where I worked on ground reaction force estimation using computer vision. Currently, I am working on finding Argumentative claims in biomedical research articles. On the application side, I am also interested in applying my skills to various science and engineering applications.
